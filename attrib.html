"Modern Jazz Samba"
Instruments: Kit, Bass, Acoustic Guitar, Piano, Flute
Feel: Bright, Driving, Bouncy
A nice little 85 BMP piece with guitar,
piano, bass, and flute. Kind of jazzy
and slightly blues-y with a more rocking beat.
The bass in this piece is actually a sample
from an 18th century double bass.

"Modern Jazz Samba" Kevin MacLeod (incompetech.com)
